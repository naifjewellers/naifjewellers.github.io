About Awa Foundation

Awa Foundation is a registered public charitable trust, since 2020. The settler of the trust and trustees are focusing to support the needy people by offering aid to construct houses, educations, financial supports to medical needs, andempower women and children and elders by offering other supports, without considering religion, cast, gender etc.
